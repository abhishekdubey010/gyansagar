More About Library

A Library is a place where there is a huge collection of books and other resources that is made accessible for reading and reference purpose. Our library was established in 2006 with donations of Lajpat Rai s/o Prem Saini of Nangal Shahidan distt Hoshairpur and his Sons Maharaj Krishan Baldev Krishan and Kewal Krishan. Its Modular and functional style fulfills a purpose of planned library.

Benefits of library

  • The library Management system is simple,user-friendly and can be easily intergrated.
  • The library management system provides mobile access to search the library catalog,schedules,books and resources from anywhere
  • Archives Preserve historic artifacts,oral histories ,digital history projects and monographs relevant to the community, including minority groups-encourage a love of reading just by being there gives children access to books.
  • It encourages interest as student's have easy access to books on hobbies, etc.
  • Library offers free educational resources to everyone.
