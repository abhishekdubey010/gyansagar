Library Policy


All the students can borrow books from the Circulation Desk located on the 1st floor. You will need
your four-digit PIN and username or your Roll No. to borrow books. Standard books are borrowed
for 2 weeks. If books are on a reading list, they are often limited to 3-day or 7-day to make sure
everyone on a course gets a chance to use them. Please note that reference material, journals
and newspapers cannot be borrowed. Students have allowance of 4 books and they have to
return or renew it within 15 days tenure.

Before returning the book you have to submit return request to the librarian. When you come with
the returning book, first you have to verified it to the librarian at the Circulation Desk. After it's
verification that this book belongs to the Library and was issued in your account, he will velidate
your request. And it will be recorded that the book has been returned in your account.

You can renew your loans at the Cirulation Desk or you can also renew books online by signing into
your account, only when you do not have any fine and the request for renewing should be within 2
weeks. Please ensure that the book is returned by the new due date. You will be fined for books
that are overdue.

It is important for all library users to be able to access the material they need. Therefore, we charge
fines on overdue items to encourage users to return material promptly. If the issued book is not
submitted on submision date,then you will be fined Rupee 2 per book.

    To avoid fines:
  • Check your due dates regularly and renew items on your account.
  • Do not share your username and password to someone else - you are responsible for the items on your account.
Note: If you owe the Library more than 100 rupee in fines, you will find that you are unable to borrow any more books, or renew the
books that you already have.

Vision

Our University library are engaged in learning and discovery as essential participants in educational Community. We develop, organize, provide access to and Preserve Materials to meet the needs of present and future generations of students. We explore and implement innovative technologies and services to deliver information and scholarly resources conveniently to users anytime anyplace. Our library will be recognized as an accessible and exceptional center of learning, research and creativity focused on student success.



Misson

Our University Library Strengthen and enhance the Teaching, Research and Services. The library promote intellutual growth and creativity by developing Collections, Facilitating access to information resources and evaluation skills and offering research assistance. Our Library provides Inspirational Environments for Collaborative and individual discovery, study and learning. We promote reading and lifelong learning.



Value

  • We recognize Users as our Primary Focus.
  • We are innovative and Proactive.
  • We support self-education, learning and research.
  • We respect our users privacy.
  • We Develop our human resources and support continuous Professional Development.
  • We aspire to be a flexible and Experimental organization that encourages innovation and staff collaboration.



Privacy Policy

It is policy of PUSSGRC University Library that Privacy of all Users will be respected in Compliance. All the library records and Other information relating to an individuals use of the library and its resources are considered Confidential. These records include but not limited to Circulation records of library materials. We Protect Each library students right to privacy and Confidentially with respect to information sought or received and resources Consulted, Borrowed, Acquired Or Transmitted.
